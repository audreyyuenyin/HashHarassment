Story Time



Filtration is a chrome extension designed by Audrey, coded by Brad and Jason, and pitched by Issac.

The four of us met at StarterHacks on March 11, 2017, at University of Waterloo.

Special thanks to Arthur, Lukas, Katrina, Jack and other mentors at this hackathon, without whom we would not be able to complete this project.


Incorporating the #HackHarassment topic, and noticing that online harassment is prevalent and noxious, we came up with Filtration in hopes of making the online community better for end users like you and I.

Maybe we cannot stop people from posting unfriendly messages, but at least we could stop reading them.

Especially for kids, we should protect them from those kind of unnecessary trash.


Together, we believe we can all make the web and the world a more peaceful place. Hope you enjoy Filtration and a more positive online community!
